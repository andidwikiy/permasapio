I have a really active mind with thousands of thoughts, but I can't afford the effort and time to put them into action.

Growing up, I really loved to write down any random thoughts that passed through my mind. I remember the time when I got my first binder, where I used to write some imaginative stories, poems, and cringey day-to-day events that I had. Time flies, The internet came to my life and and slowly shifted from my beloved binder to blogger. I was in Juior High when I create my first blogger.com account to write some things about my favorite game at that time (i still have the website anw üòÅ).

In high school, I once again shifted from blogger.com to tumblr as I found it easier to post directly from Instagram, plus the tumblr community was a thing back then. At this point in my life, I technically wrote everything via Instagram, posted it all at once to Tumblr, and deleted the Instagram post because I didn't really want anyone to read my writings. Unfortunately, in 2016, Tumblr got banned in Indonesia. Therefore, I stopped writing stories and thoughts. Althogh, the ban has been lifted, I lost access to my account and couldn't even find it if I searched it. https://andidwiki.tumblr.com/.

I'm glad that I gained the motivation to once again write down everything that I experienced. To write down my feelings, emotions, journey, you name it!. is cheaper than therapy, ya know.
